We have added integration with [**HytaleModding Wiki**](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/) to Modifold.

If your mod already has a wiki on [**HytaleModding Wiki**](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/), you can now quickly attach it to your project on Modifold. After that, your project will get a dedicated **Wiki** tab where synced pages and Markdown content will be displayed.

## What this gives you

- A dedicated Wiki tab directly on your project page on Modifold
- Synced pages from [HytaleModding Wiki](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/)
- Markdown content displayed on Modifold without manual copying
- More convenient navigation for players, testers, and other modders

## How to connect it

Everything is very simple to set up: just open your project settings on Modifold and add a link to your wiki from [HytaleModding Wiki](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/).

After saving, Modifold will use that wiki as the content source for the **Wiki** tab inside your project.

## Where to edit the wiki

Important: configuring the structure and editing the pages themselves still happens on the [HytaleModding Wiki](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/) website.

In this case, Modifold acts as an integration and display point, so your project's documentation is available to users right next to the main mod page.
