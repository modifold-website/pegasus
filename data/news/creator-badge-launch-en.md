## A new step for creators on Modifold

We wanted creator status to be clear, fair, and useful for the community.
So today we are moving away from blue checkmarks and introducing a new format: **Creator Badge**.

This is not about being "elite". It is about real contribution: publishing projects, staying active, and building trust with players.

## What is Creator Badge

**Creator Badge** is an official status for active and trusted mod creators.

If you have the badge, it:

- highlights your profile and comments
- helps players quickly find trusted creators
- gives you better chances to appear in picks, homepage blocks, and creator support campaigns

## What changed

- We removed the old blue checkmark system
- We removed manual verification moderation
- We added a new path: **Settings → Creator Badge**
- You can now see requirements and your progress right away

## Badge requirements

To activate Creator Badge, you need to:

- **Publish 3 projects**
- **Reach 500 total downloads** across your published projects

When both requirements are met, you can activate the badge with a button in settings.

![](https://media.modifold.com/news/creator_badge_2.png)

## Why this approach

We want this system to be:

- transparent — clear requirements for everyone
- fast — no waiting for manual review
- fair — status is based on real creator contribution

This is a step toward a stronger ecosystem where not only mods, but also the people behind them get recognition.

## What’s next

We will keep improving creator profiles, stats, and promotion tools so strong creators can get more visibility and support.

Thanks for building Modifold with us.
